You already know how to write a great program. You already know what to recommend when a client hits a plateau. You already know how to answer a DM at 9pm. That knowledge lives in your head — and you retype it every time.

In one working session, we encode it once. Plans, recommendations, and replies start from your standard instead of a blank page. You spend the saved time with clients, not at a laptop.
