How it works

Observe. Encode. Review. Grow.

One short loop turns a routine into something the agent can run beside you. The motion is the same for every skill — from invoice collection to customer check-ins to the financial advice a CFO would give.

1Observe

Watch the work.

Pick one routine you do every week or quarter. Link plays back the wallet activity around it — vendors, amounts, follow-ups, exceptions.

InputWallet activity
2Encode

Capture the rules.

Write down what you actually do, in plain conditions and examples. The skill becomes a small, readable manual — not a black box.

OutputCallable skill
3Review

Check the output.

The skill prepares each action and queues it for your approval. You confirm, edit, or reject. Every call sharpens the next one.

CadenceDaily / weekly
4Grow

Reuse the skill.

Once it runs reliably, hand it to a teammate, a virtual assistant, or another product surface. The same routine, encoded once.

Reach1 → many
