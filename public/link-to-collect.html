Link Collect creates an invoice archive from Link activity without changing who issues the invoice. Each payment keeps the merchant, card, amount, tax treatment, and source document in one place.

It works across recurring subscriptions, usage-based tools, seats, credits, and one-off purchases. When Stripe already hosts the invoice, Link can attach it; when it doesn’t, Link can request it from the merchant.

Customers get a searchable archive. Merchants get fewer invoice support requests. Accountants get original documents and a clean CSV.

The wallet organizes how people pay. Link Collect organizes what finance needs after payment.
